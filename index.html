Our Commitment to Your Pet’s Well-being

At AL-Rehman Veterinary Clinic, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care for your beloved animals. Our experienced team uses advanced techniques and a compassionate approach to ensure every pet receives personalized attention and treatment. From routine check-ups to complex surgeries, we are here to support you and your furry family members at every stage of their lives. Your pet’s health is our top priority.

About Our Clinic

Al Rehman Veterinary Clinic, established in 2024, is dedicated to providing the highest quality veterinary care for your beloved pets. We have been serving the Lahore community with a commitment to excellence.

We understand that your pet is a part of your family, and we treat every patient with the same love and attention we would our own. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to provide comprehensive care, from routine check-ups to advanced medical procedures.

Our Services

Preventative Care

Regular check-ups and wellness exams are key to a long and healthy life. We offer comprehensive wellness exams, vaccinations, parasite prevention, and nutritional counseling.

Surgery

Our surgical suite is equipped for both routine and advanced procedures. Our team prioritizes safety, pain management, and a smooth recovery for every patient.

Dental Care

Proper dental hygiene is crucial. We provide professional teeth cleaning, polishing, and dental extractions to prevent and treat oral diseases.

Diagnostics

With in-house lab work and advanced digital X-ray technology, we can quickly and accurately diagnose medical conditions to begin treatment as soon as possible.

Livestock Farming

We offer expert veterinary services for livestock, including health management, disease prevention, and nutritional guidance to ensure the productivity and well-being of your farm animals.

Artificial Insemination

Our clinic provides professional artificial insemination services to help improve the genetic quality and breeding efficiency of your livestock.
