Our History

AL-Rehman Veterinary Clinic was established in 2024 in Lahore, Pakistan, with the goal of providing top-notch veterinary services to the local community. Located at Kahna Nau, Ferozpur Road, Lahore, our clinic has quickly become a trusted name for compassionate and professional pet care.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional veterinary care by combining advanced medical practices with a deep sense of compassion, ensuring the health and happiness of every animal that walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be the leading veterinary clinic in Lahore, recognized for our unwavering commitment to animal welfare and our role as a community partner in promoting responsible pet ownership.

Our Facilities

  • Advanced surgical suite for a wide range of procedures
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic imaging (X-rays, ultrasound)
  • Modern dental care services
  • Comfortable and safe boarding facilities for pets
  • Dedicated consultation rooms for thorough examinations
  • In-house laboratory for quick and accurate test results
